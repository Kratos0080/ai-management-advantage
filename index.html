The Management Trinity 管理三支柱

Three skills that determine AI outcomes 决定AI成效的三项关键能力

Delegation 授权

Decompose complex work into discrete, well-scoped tasks an AI agent can own end-to-end. Not 'write me a report' — but 'reconcile Q4 costs across three entities using GAAP, flag variances above 5%.' 将复杂工作拆解为AI代理可端到端承担的明确任务。不是'帮我写份报告'——而是'按GAAP标准核对三个实体的Q4成本，标记偏差超过5%的项目。'

Clear Briefing 清晰指示

Provide the full context an agent needs: constraints, examples, standards, institutional knowledge. This is not prompt engineering — it is management communication, encoded for machines. 提供代理执行所需的完整上下文：约束条件、示例、标准、机构知识。这不是 Prompt Engineering——而是面向机器编码的管理沟通。

Quality Evaluation 质量评估

Judge output against professional standards before deployment. Domain expertise — knowing what 'good' looks like in your function — is the irreplaceable human layer. 在部署前依据专业标准判断产出质量。领域专长——知道你所在职能中'好'的标准——是不可替代的人类判断层。
